I was born in Naples, Southern Italy. I am fluent in Italian, English and Bulgarian. In 2022, I was awarded the position of Research Fellow at the Faculty of Social Sciences of the University of Ljubljana (Slovenia). Before that, I was a senior risk analyst at Global Risk Insights, where I started as an occasional contributor. I started publishing in websites and journals on culture, society and politics of Southeast Europe and the former USSR. Between 2015 and 2017, I won several awards in nationwide literary competitions.


Besides being a researcher, I occasionally give guest lectures and regularly speak at international conferences on the same topics.
