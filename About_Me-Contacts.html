About Me

I was born in Naples, Southern Italy. I am fluent in Italian, English and Bulgarian. In 2022 I got the post of Research Assistant at the Faculty of Social Sciences of the University of Ljubljana (Slovenia). Before that, I was a Senior Risk Analyst at Global Risk Insights, where I started as an occasional contributor. I started publishing on websites and magazines about the culture, society and politics of South Eastern Europe and the former USSR. Between 2015 and 2017 I won several prizes in nation-wide literary contests.


Besides being a researcher, I give occasional guest lectures and speak regularly at international conferences on these same topics.
